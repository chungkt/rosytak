Driving Directions

From Eastern Harbor Tunnel: when you are out of Eastern Tunnel, follow signage to Kwun Tong. Take the third exit from the first round-about you come across. Keep right. Go up Hip Wo Street until you hit Tsui Ping Road. Turn right into Tsui Ping Road and the car park is on your left.

From Kwun Tong: Drive along Kwun Tong Road until you hit first round-about. Take the first exit. Keep right. Go up Hip Wo Street until you hit Tsui Ping Road. Turn right into Tsui Ping Road and the car park is on your left.
